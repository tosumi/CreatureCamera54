About this app

CreatureCamera is a fun camera app where mysterious creatures appear in your photos. Press the shutter and discover fantasy creatures living in your world!

How to use

How do I take a photo?

Tap the shutter button on the main screen. A creature may appear in your photo!

How do I unlock more frames?

Additional frame types are unlocked as you take more photos. Keep shooting to discover them all!

Why does the app need camera access?

Camera access is required to take photos. The app does not store or share your photos outside of your device without your permission.

Where are my photos saved?

Photos are saved to your device's camera roll. The app does not upload photos to any server.

Privacy

CreatureCamera does not collect personal data. Camera access is used solely for taking photos within the app. Photos remain on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.
